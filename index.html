📖 Cricket Scoring Rules 🏏

🎯 Basic Scoring:

  • 🔢 Runs: Click 0️⃣, 1️⃣, 2️⃣, 3️⃣, 4️⃣, or 6️⃣ to add runs
  • ↔️ Wides (WD): +1 run, doesn't count as a ball 🚫
  • 🚫 No Balls (NB): +1 run, free hit next ball 🎯
  • 🏃 Byes (BYE): +1 run, counts as a ball ⚾
  • 🦵 Leg Byes (LB): +1 run, counts as a ball ⚾

🎯 Wicket Types:

  • 💥 Bowled: Ball smashes the stumps 🎳
  • ✋ Caught: Fielder takes the catch 🧤
  • 🦵 LBW: Leg before wicket decision 👨‍⚖️
  • 🏃 Run Out: Batsman falls short 🚨
  • 🧤 Stumped: Keeper whips off bails ⚡
  • 💢 Hit Wicket: Batsman's own mistake 😅

⏱️ Match Flow:

  • ⏳ Each over = 6 balls 🔄
  • 🔄 Switch innings after all overs or 10 wickets ☝️
  • 🏆 Limited overs: Most runs wins 🎉
  • 🧐 Test matches: Most runs over two innings wins 📊
  • 🎯 Target: Chasing team must reach set runs 🏃‍♂️

💡 Pro Tips:

  • ↩️ Use UNDO to correct mistakes ✏️
  • 📊 Check STATS for match analysis 📈
  • 💾 SAVE match data for records 🗃️
  • 🎉 Celebrate big moments! 🎊
